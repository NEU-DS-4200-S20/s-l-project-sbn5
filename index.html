Motivation

SBN's core missions is to build a community that can support a culture of sustainability and local food. There are many organizations that care about local food, but it can be difficult for them to know that they have many other similar-minded organizations nearby.

SBN has a member directory containing information about all buyers, vendors, and other organizations. They wanted to build a visualization that will go into marketing materials and on their website to communicate the scope of their work and to connect members of the local food community.

Expectation: SBN's existing database was not a pretty sight, and they did not find it visually appealing enough to display on their website. They wanted a visualization that would target business who were interested in joining the network and seeing who else is involved. What they were specifically looking for was a list + visualization to represent all the existing businesses in the network.

Visualization explanation

Final visualization screenshots (PNG images), design justifications, UI walk-through, and linked presentation slides.

One of SBN's core missions is to build a community that can support a culture of sustainability and shopping local. To do this, SBN has a member directory containing information about all buyers, vendors, and other organizations, but they do not have an easy way to look all of their members, especially in regards to their specialty and location.

For our project, we decided to create a visualization that allows viewers to see not only where members are located and what their focus is, but also allows for customization of what they would like to see (filtering by any mix of SBN member attributes).

Data Preprocessing

The dataset we were given had the information for a little over 100 members. To make the data useable, we had to clean it up and properly categorize each member organization. This required us to research any gaps in the data (for example, organizations that did not have an address/location in the dataset), as well as to categorize each member based on what they sell or are focused on (catering, beverages, consulting, green energy, etc.)
